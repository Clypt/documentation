---

<Warning>
  When configuring API permissions, **never** enable any "Withdraw" or "Deposit" permissions. ClyptAI only requires read access and trade execution.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to API Management">
    Log in to your Kraken Pro account. Click on your **Profile Icon** in the top right corner, select **Settings**, and then click **Connection & API**.

    <Frame>
      ![Kraken Settings](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at2.48.17PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure API Permissions">
    Click the purple **Create API Key** button to start generating a new key for ClyptAI.

    In the description field, give your key a recognizable name (e.g., "ClyptAI Trading").

    Scroll down and carefully configure the permissions:

    1. **Funds Permissions** - Check the **Query** box.
    2. **Orders and Trades** - Check **all 4** options.
    3. Scroll down to **IP Address Restriction** and turn the toggle **ON**.

    <Frame>
      ![API Permissions](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at2.53.25PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy IP Whitelist from ClyptAI">
    Keep your Kraken tab open.

    1. Open a new browser tab and log in to your **ClyptAI** dashboard.
    2. Navigate to the exchange connection page and select **Kraken** from the dropdown menu.

    <Frame>
      ![ClyptAI Dashboard](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at3.08.22PM.png)
    </Frame>

    3. Locate the **IP whitelist** box and click the **Copy** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste IP and Generate Key">
    1. Go back to your **Kraken** tab.
    2. Paste the copied IP whitelist into the text box under the IP address restriction section.

    <Warning>
      Do **not** check any boxes related to "Withdraw" or "Deposit". ClyptAI will never request withdrawal access.
    </Warning>

    <Frame>
      ![IP Restriction](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at3.15.33PM.png)
    </Frame>

    3. Click the purple **Generate Key** button at the bottom of the page.
    4. A "Verify it's you" pop-up will appear. Complete the security check using your Passkey or Authenticator app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Keys and Connect to ClyptAI">
    Once verified, your newly generated keys will be displayed. The keys are only shown **once**.

    <Info>
      **Immediately** copy your API Key and Secret Key. Paste them securely into your personal notes or directly into the ClyptAI connection screen.
    </Info>

    1. Go back to your open **ClyptAI** tab.
    2. Copy the **API Key** from Kraken and paste it into the API Key field in ClyptAI.
    3. Copy the **Private Key** from Kraken and paste it into the API Secret field in ClyptAI.
    4. Click the **Connect** button to finish.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the Connection">
    To confirm the integration is working:

    In the top right corner of the dashboard page, you will now see the **Connected Exchange** status.

    Your Kraken account is now securely integrated and ready for automated execution. You can now start deploying your alpha.

    <Frame>
      ![Connected](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at3.29.19PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
