---
title: "Kraken"
---

<Warning>
  When configuring API permissions, NEVER enable "Withdraw Funds". ClyptAI only requires read access and trade execution permissions.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Navigate to API Management">
    Log in to your Kraken Pro account. Click on yyour **Profile Icon** in the top right corner, select **Settings**, and then click on **Connection & API**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 2 48 17 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at2.48.17PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Configure API Permissions">
    Click the purple **Create API Key** button to start generating a new key for ClyptAI.

    In the description field, give your key recognizable name (e.g., 'ClyptAI Trading').

    Scroll down to carefully configure the permissions.

    1. Funds Permissions: Check the **Query** box. 
    2. Orders and trades: Check **ALL 4** Options.
    3. Scroll down to the **IP Address Restriction** section and turn the toggle **ON**.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 2 53 25 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at2.53.25PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Copy IP Whitelist from ClyptAI">
    Before you can generate the key, you need the authorized IP addresses from ClyptAI.

    _(Please keep your Kraken tab open!)_

    1. Open a new browser tab and log in to your **ClyptAI** dashboard.
    2. Navigate to the exchange connection page and select **Kraken** from the dropdown menu.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 3 08 22 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at3.08.22PM.png)
    </Frame>
    3. Locate the **IP whitelist** box and click the **Copy** buttion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Paste IP & Generate Key">
    1. Go back to your **Kraken** tab.
    2. **Paste** the copied IP Whitelist into the text box under the IP address restriction section.

    <Warning>
      **Security Note:** Absolutely do NOT check any boxes related to "Withdraw" or "Deposit". ClyptAI is built with strict security measures and will never ask for withdrawal permission. Protect your assets.
    </Warning>
    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 3 15 33 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at3.15.33PM.png)
    </Frame>
    3. Click the purple **Generate Key** button at the bottom of the page.
    4. A **Verify it's you** pop-up will appear. Complete the security check using your Passkey or Authenticator app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Save your Keys & Connect to ClyptAI">
    Once verified, an "API Key created" dialog box will appear showing your newly generated keys. Please pay close attention to this step, as your keys will only be deplayed **once** for security reasons.

    <Info>
      Copy Your Keys: Once verified, your 'API Key' and 'Secret Key' will be generated and displayed on the screen. **Immediately** copy both keys. Paste them securely into your personal notes or directly into the ClyptAI connection screen.
    </Info>
    1. Go back to your open **ClyptAI** tab.
    2. Click the copy icon next to the **API Key** on Kraken and paste it into the API Key field in ClyptAI.
    3. Click the copy icon next to the **Private Key** on Kraken and paste it into the API Secret field in ClyptAI.
    4. Click the **Connect** button in ClyptAI to finish!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6: Verify the Connection">
    Congratulations! You have successfully linked your Kraken account to ClyptAI.

    To confirm that the integration is working perfectly:

    In the top right corner of the dashboard page, you will now see **Connected Exchange"** status.

    Your Kraken account is now securely integrated and ready for automated execution.

    You can now start deploying your alpha!

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 3 29 19 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at3.29.19PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
