---
title: "Coinbase"
---

<Warning>
  When configuring API permissions, NEVER enable "Withdraw Funds". ClyptAI only requires read access and trade execution permissions.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Navigate to API Management">
    Log in to your Coinbase account. Click on yyour **Profile Icon** in the top right corner, select **Settings**.

    On the left Sidebar menu, scroll down and click on **API.**

    ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 4 01 23 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at4.01.23PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Create API Key">
    Click the blue **Create API Key** button to start generating a new key for ClyptAI.

    ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 4 03 18 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at4.03.18PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Configure Basic Settings & Permissions.">
    In the "Create API Key" pop-up window, configure the following settings:

    1. API Key Nickname: Give your key a recognizable name (e.g., 'ClyptAI').
    2. Portfolio: Leave it as 'Primary'.
    3. Permissions: Check the first two boxes:
       - View (Read-only)
       - Trade (Execute trades on your behalf)

    _(Wait! Do not click the create button yet. You need to restrict the IP first.)_

    ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 4 08 09 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at4.08.09PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Copy IP Whitelist from ClyptAI">
    Before finalizing the key, you must retrieve the authorized IP addresses from ClyptAI.

    1. Open a new browser tab and log in to your **ClyptAI** dashboard.
    2. Navigate to the exchange connection page and select **Coinbase** from the dropdown menu.
    3. Locate the **IP whitelist** box and click the **Copy** button.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 4 11 13 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at4.11.13PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Paste IP & Generate Key">
    1. Go back to your **Coinbase** tab.
    2. **Paste** the copied IP whitelist into the **IP allowlist** text box.
    3. Click the blue **Create & Download** button at the bottom. Complete any 2FA security checks if prompted.

    <Warning>
      **Security Note:** Absolutely do NOT check the **"Transfer (initiate transfer of funds)"** permission. ClyptAI is built with strict security measures and will never ask for withdrawal permissions. Protect your assets.
    </Warning>
    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 4 15 29 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at4.15.29PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6: Save your Keys & Connect to ClyptAI">
    Coinbase will generate your API credentials.

    Once generated, an "API Key created" dialog box will appear showing your newly generated keys. Please pay close attention to this step, as your keys will only be deplayed **once** for security reasons.

    <Info>
      Copy Your Keys: Once verified, your 'API Key' and 'Secret Key' will be generated and displayed on the screen. **Immediately** copy both keys. Paste them securely into your personal notes or directly into the ClyptAI connection screen.
    </Info>
    1. Go back to your open **ClyptAI** tab.
    2. Copy the **API Key name** from Coinbase and paste it into the **API Key** field in ClyptAI.
    3. Copy the **Private Key** from Coinbase and paste it into the **API Secret** field in ClyptAI
    4. Click the **Connect** button to finish!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 7: Verify the Connection">
    Congratulations! You have successfully linked your Coinbase account to ClyptAI.

    To confirm that the integration is working perfectly:

    In the top right corner of the dashboard page, you will now see **Connected Exchange"** status.

    Your Coinbase account is now securely integrated and ready for automated execution.

    You can now start deploying your alpha!

    You can now start deploying your alpha!

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 04 At 4 38 29 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-04at4.38.29PM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
