<Warning>
  When configuring API permissions, **never** enable "Enable Withdrawals". ClyptAI only requires read access and trade execution permissions.
</Warning>

<Info>
  **Skip the API Setup with Fast Connect!**

  As an official broker partner with **Bybit**, ClyptAI allows you to skip the manual API key generation entirely. Just select the exchange on your ClyptAI dashboard, click **Fast Connect**, and log in to link your account instantly!
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Navigate to Bybit API Management">
    1. Log in to your **Bybit** account.
    1. Hover over your **Profile icon** in the top right corner and select **API** from the dropdown menu.
    1. Click the orange **Create New Key** button located on the top right side.
    1. A pop-up will appear. Select the **System-generated API Keys** option.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 05 At 11 20 47 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-05at11.20.47AM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Copy IP Whitelist from ClyptAI">
    Before filling out the rest of the Bybit form, let's grab the authorized IPs.

    _(Please keep your Bybit tab open!)_

    1. Open a new browser tab and log in to your **ClyptAI** dashboard.
    2. Navigate to the exchange connection page and select **Bybit** from the dropdown menu.
    3. Locate the **IP Whitelist** box and click the **Copy** button.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 05 At 11 22 42 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-05at11.22.42AM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Paste IP & Configure Settings">
    1. Go back to your **Bybit** tab.
    2. Ensure **API Transaction** is selected under "API Key Usage".
    3. Enter a name for your API key in the **Name for the API key** box (e.g., `ClyptAI`).
    4. Under "API Key Permissions", strictly select **Read-Write**.
    5. In the IP restriction section, select **"Only IPs with permissions granted are allowed to access the OpenAPI"** and **Paste** the IP Whitelist you copied from ClyptAI.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 05 At 11 24 56 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-05at11.24.56AM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Select Trading Permissions">
    Now, scroll down to the permissions checklist. Under the **Unified Trading** section, check **ONLY** the following boxes:

    ✅ Under **Contract**: Check `Orders` and `Positions`.

    ✅ Under **SPOT**: Check `Trade`.

    <Warning>
      **Security Note:** Scroll down to the **Assets** section and make sure absolutely NOTHING is checked (such as Account Transfer, Withdrawal, etc.). ClyptAI only requires trade execution access. Your funds remain securely in your Bybit wallet.
    </Warning>
    Click the Orange **Submit** button at the bottom. Complete your security  verification (2FA/Email code) to generate the keys.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Save Your Keys & Connect to ClyptAI">
    <Warning>
      **CRITICAL:** Bybit will only show your **API Secret** ONCE. If you click "Understood" and close the pop-up, the secret key will be hidden forever. Please paste it into ClyptAI immediately before closing the window!
    </Warning>
    Bybit will now display a pop-up with your newly generated keys.

    1. Go back to your open **ClyptAI** tab.
    2. Copy the `API Key`from Bybit and paste it into the API Key field in ClyptAI.
    3. Copy the `API Secret` from Bybit and paste it into the `API Secret` field in ClyptAI.
    4. Click the **Connect** button to finish!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6: Verify the Connection">
    Congratulations! You have successfully linked your Bybit account.

    To confirm that the integration is working perfectly:

    1. In the "Connect to your exchange accounts" page, you will now see a green **Connected** status next to Bybit.
    2. Head over to your main ClyptAI **Dashboard**. Under the **Connected Exchanges** section, you should clearly see the Bybit logo displayed.

    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot 2026 03 05 At 11 32 48 AM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-05at11.32.48AM.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
